## Where to source CC0 audio
- **Freesound.org** — filter by the CC0 tag: <https://freesound.org/browse/tags/cc0/>
- **Pixabay** sound effects (CC0, no attribution): <https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/>
- **ZapSplat** — CC0 1.0 license type (free account)

Good diesel *loops* that pitch-shift cleanly across RPM are rare on CC0 sites — this
is exactly why the pack is pluggable and the real value is the swap engine, not the
starter audio. Own recordings (48 kHz, clean loop points per RPM band) are the
premium path.
